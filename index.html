Mathematics & Research

I completed my PhD in mathematics at UC Davis under the supervision of Professor Adam Jacob. I completed my BAs in mathematics and statistics at UC Berkeley.

My research is in differential geometry. My dissertation explored the deformed Hermitian-Yang-Mills equation with Calabi Ansatz. I am currently interested in how differential geometry appears in biological phenomenon. 

Areas of interest:
• differential geometry
• differential equations
• computational biology
